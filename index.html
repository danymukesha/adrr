Databases & Data Repositories

Alzheimer's Disease Specific

  • Alzheimer's Disease Neuroimaging Initiative (ADNI)

    Longitudinal multicenter study designed to develop clinical, imaging, genetic, and biochemical biomarkers for the early detection and tracking of Alzheimer's disease.

  • Alzheimer's Disease Sequencing Project (ADSP)

    Large-scale sequencing analysis of genomes of individuals with Alzheimer's disease.

  • AlzGPS

    A genome-wide positioning systems platform for Alzheimer's disease drug discovery.

  • AlzPED

    Alzheimer's Preclinical Efficacy Database for evaluation of preclinical therapy development.

General Neuroscience

  • Allen Brain Atlas

    Comprehensive atlas of gene expression in the adult and developing brain.

  • Human Brain Project

    European research initiative aiming to build a collaborative ICT-based scientific research infrastructure.
